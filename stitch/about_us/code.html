About Sportsman’s HQ

Sportsman’s HQ is a local outdoor store in Minong, Wisconsin. We focus on dependable gear, practical advice, and service that supports fishing, hunting, camping, apparel, and docks & lifts.

Inside Sportsman’s HQ

Archery & Hunting

  • New and used guns
  • Ammunition and accessories
  • Mathews, Parker, and Hoyt bows
  • Archery department with a bow repair center
  • Tree stands, blaze orange, camouflage clothing
  • WI DNR registration station and hunting licenses

Fishing

  • Large fishing gear and tackle selection
  • Live bait
  • Ice fishing supplies (including fish shacks)
  • Wisconsin fishing licenses

Camping, Etc.

  • Outdoor footwear and clothing
  • Camping equipment

Docks & Lifts

  • Roll out and floating docks
  • Pier Pleasure dealer support
  • Porta-Docks dealer support
  • DockRite waterfront systems
  • Boat lifts
  • Pontoon lifts and PWC lifts
  • Sectional, roll-in, multi, and post dock options

Welcome & Apparel Entry

The front area mixes apparel, seasonal displays, and quick access to hunting and camping gear.

Main Floor & In-Store Bar

A central gathering spot with bar seating, gift items, and broad access to gear throughout the store.

Hunting & Archery

Dedicated hunting aisles include clothing, accessories, archery equipment, and the firearm counter.

Fishing Tackle Floor

Large tackle sections, rod displays, aisle-by-aisle organization, and seasonal inventory for open water and ice.

Marine, Camping & Service Areas

Specialized areas for line spooling, life jackets, safety equipment, and cold-storage bait and drinks.
